Our Story

Built on a commitment to biological excellence

Founded in the United States, American Biosources Inc. emerged from a shared vision: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge science and reliable pharmaceutical supply chains. From our earliest days sourcing premium human-derived biological materials, we have remained steadfast in our pursuit of purity, traceability, and scientific integrity.

Today, our state-of-the-art facilities operate under rigorous regulatory frameworks — serving global biotech firms, research institutions, and pharmaceutical companies who depend on materials that meet the highest quality standards. Every vial, every sample, every shipment carries the promise of American Biosources.

What Drives Us

Core Values that define
every decision we make

At American Biosources, our principles aren't framed on walls — they're embedded in every protocol, every partnership, and every product we release to the world.

01

Uncompromising Quality

Every biological material leaving our facility undergoes multi-stage QC verification. We hold ourselves to standards that exceed regulatory minimums — because lives depend on it.

02

Scientific Innovation

Continuous investment in R&D keeps our methodologies at the forefront. We evolve alongside the science we serve.

03

Ethical Sourcing

Our supply chain adheres to strict ethical and informed-consent frameworks. We believe transparency is the foundation of trust.

04

Regulatory Compliance

Full FDA, GMP, and ISO conformance isn't a checkbox — it's a living commitment that shapes daily operations and long-term strategy.

05

Client Partnership

We view our clients as partners in progress — offering custom formulations, technical consultation, and responsive support that go far beyond transactional interactions.

06

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is woven into our manufacturing and logistics operations — reducing our footprint while expanding our impact on human health.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our path

1999

Company Founded

American Biosources Inc. is established with a clear mission: to supply the biopharmaceutical industry with the highest-quality human biological materials available.

2004

First GMP Certification

Achieved Good Manufacturing Practice certification, signaling our transition from a regional supplier to a compliant, audited biopharmaceutical partner.

2009

International Expansion

Extended our distribution network to 20+ countries across Europe and Asia, establishing cold-chain logistics that maintain product integrity across hemispheres.

2015

ISO 9001 Accreditation

Earned ISO 9001 certification, formalizing our quality management system and reinforcing client confidence in our processes at every level.

2020

COVID-19 Response Contribution

Played a critical role supplying serum and plasma materials to leading vaccine developers during the global pandemic — a proud chapter in our history.

2024

New R&D Facility Launch

Opened our advanced biopharmaceutical R&D center, tripling our custom formulation capacity and enabling next-generation cell and gene therapy material development.
