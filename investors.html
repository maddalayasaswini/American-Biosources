Why Invest

The Investment Thesis

American BioSources stands at the intersection of cutting-edge science and commercial execution — backed by a proven leadership team and a diversified portfolio across oncology, rare diseases, and immunology.

First-in-Class Pipeline

Proprietary molecular scaffolds target high-value indications with limited competition, offering multiple shots on goal across 18+ active programs from discovery through Phase III.

Deep IP Moat

Over 200 granted patents and a robust prosecution strategy ensure 12–15 year exclusivity windows on our core programs, protecting long-term revenue streams.

Accelerating Revenue

Two commercial-stage products generating >$900M in annual net revenue, with CAGR guidance of 55–65% through 2027 driven by label expansions and new launches.

Global Commercial Reach

Commercial partnerships spanning 40+ countries, with a fully-owned US sales force of 1,200 representatives and co-promotion agreements across Europe and Asia-Pacific.

Fortress Balance Sheet

$1.8B in cash and equivalents, zero debt, and disciplined capital allocation policy — providing the financial flexibility to fund organic growth and strategic acquisitions.

Experienced Leadership

Management team with combined 150+ years in biopharma, responsible for 7 prior FDA approvals and more than $12B in value creation across prior companies.
